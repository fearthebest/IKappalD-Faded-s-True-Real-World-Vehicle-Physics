title=IKappaID & Faded's True Real World Vehicle Physics
description=IKappaID & Faded's True Real World Vehicle Physics
description=
description=Project Zomboid’s vehicles are useful, dangerous, noisy, and sometimes a little too floaty. This mod aims to make driving feel more grounded, believable, and satisfying without turning vehicles into arcade machines or overpowered survival tools.
description=
description=True Real World Vehicle Physics adjusts vanilla Build 42.18+ vehicle mass and engine force values so cars, vans, trucks, and heavier vehicles feel closer to what you would expect from their real-world counterparts. Small cars should feel lighter and easier to move, while vans and heavy vehicles should feel like they actually have weight behind them.
description=
description=The goal is simple: better vehicle feel while staying clean, stable, and multiplayer-friendly.
description=
description=What this mod changes:
description=- Tunes vanilla vehicle mass values
description=- Tunes engine force for more believable acceleration
description=- Gives heavier vehicles a more grounded driving feel
description=- Helps reduce the “floaty” feeling some vehicles can have
description=- Full Kiurio / KI5 vehicle pack roster support (442 vehicles)
description=- Keeps other unknown modded vehicles untouched by default
description=- Includes sandbox options for additional tuning
description=
description=Compatibility and safety:
description=This is a clean-room Lua-based tuning mod. It does not replace core Java classes, does not overwrite broad vanilla UI systems, and does not try to take control away from the server in multiplayer.
description=
description=The mod is designed with multiplayer in mind. Vehicle tuning is deterministic and shared, with the server treated as the authority for vehicle behavior.
description=
description=Common Sense Reborn compatibility:
description=This mod is designed to work alongside Common Sense Reborn. When Common Sense Reborn is active, this mod avoids touching CSR-owned systems, keys, UI hooks, inventory behavior, hotwire logic, seatbelt hooks, timed actions, and other unrelated features.
description=
description=Recommended load order:
description=1. Common Sense Reborn
description=2. IKappaID & Faded's True Real World Vehicle Physics
description=3. Project Faded Car (optional companion; engine bay service + IKFRVP physics bridge)
description=
description=Sandbox options:
description=Server owners and players can adjust several tuning values through sandbox settings, including general power and mass scaling. By default, unknown third-party vehicles are left alone so other vehicle mods are not unexpectedly changed.
description=
